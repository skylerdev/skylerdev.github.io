Chipless Support

Quick help for running chipless poker games.

Quick Answers

  • Change blinds: In the lobby, edit the small blind; big blind updates automatically.
  • Player limit: Chipless supports 3–8 players. Trim your lobby list if you exceed the cap.
  • Rotate dealer/blinds: Start a new hand and Chipless will move dealer, small blind, and big blind for you.
  • Undo a mistake: If something goes wrong, restore the previous hand from the game view.
  • No chips showing? Make sure each player has chips > 0; busted players move to spectators on the next hand.
