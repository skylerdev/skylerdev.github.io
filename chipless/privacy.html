Chipless Privacy Policy

Effective January 2026

What Chipless Does

Chipless helps you run home poker games without physical chips. The app keeps track of players, blinds, bets, and pots on your device.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers. Chipless stores only player names locally on your device via UserDefaults. No analytics, ads, or third-party SDKs are included.

How Data Is Used

Player names are used solely to label seats and persist your lobby between sessions. This data stays on your device unless you share it.

Data Sharing

No personal or gameplay data is transmitted to us or to third parties.

Your Choices

  • Remove saved names by clearing them in the lobby and starting a new game.
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data.
