Chipless Privacy Policy

Effective February 2026

What Chipless Does

Chipless helps you run home poker games without physical chips. The app keeps track of players, blinds, bets, and pots. You can play locally on one device or create an online game that others can join with a short game code.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. The only information sent to our server is the one-line username you choose when joining an online game. This username is held in memory for the duration of the game and discarded when you leave. No analytics, ads, or third-party SDKs are included.

For local games, player names are stored on your device only.

Online Multiplayer

When you create or join an online game, your chosen username and in-game actions (bets, folds, etc.) are transmitted to our server to sync the game with other players. This data is ephemeral — it exists only while the game is active and is permanently deleted when the game ends or you leave.

We do not store game history, player statistics, or any other data from online sessions.

Data Sharing

No personal or gameplay data is shared with third parties.

Your Choices

  • Remove saved names by clearing them in the lobby and starting a new game.
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data.
