//: About me

Hi! I'm Skyler Devlaming, a Developer, Musician, and Athlete in Vancouver, BC. I've been programming ever since I could use a terminal, and started formally learning in high school, where I took AP CompSci A. I graduated from the British Columbia Institute of Technology in 2020, and now work on my own time as an iOS Developer.

I previously worked at Provincial Health Services Authority as a technical analyst for an app called Procura, which scheduled healthcare workers providing at home services to patients.

I enjoy programming because I recieve fulfillment from filling a need to end users, whether that be through entertainment or utility. My goal is to continue developing iOS apps that make people happy!
